# Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark cinematic athlete documentary with arena haze, warm film grain, deep wine-red shadows, restrained gold data accents, editorial portrait framing, and tactile scoreboard details. The emotional register moves from public scrutiny to earned calm. Original AI artwork replaces broadcast footage.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#0D0B0E`
- Wine shadow: `#3A101C`
- Warm paper text: `#F5ECDD`
- Championship gold: `#F3C966`
- Muted steel: `#A8A0A5`

## Typography

- Chinese and narrative copy: `Microsoft YaHei`, 350/900 weight contrast
- Numbers and metadata: `Bahnschrift`, tabular numerals

## Motion

- High-energy moments use 0.2-0.35s confident entrances.
- Reflective moments use 0.55-0.8s fades and slow image pushes.
- Crossfades are the primary transition; one fast gold flash marks the 2016 turning point.
- Every scene has one ambient motion only.

## What NOT to Do

- No NBA or team logos, copied uniforms, broadcast frames, or watermarks.
- No neon cyan/purple gradients or generic card dashboards.
- No empty pure-black frames or centered web-page stacks.
- No more than three statistics in one scene.
- No literal photo collage or fake news layout.
